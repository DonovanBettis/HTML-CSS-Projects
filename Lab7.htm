Visiting Space Center Houston should be on your list if your coming down to Houston.
It's not too expensive and it can fill up a lot of your time all while keeping you entertained!








The second place to visit would be the Houston Museum of Natural Science.
General Admission tickts start at $13.75 per adult so it's inexpensive and it allows you to learn a lot and see some interesting artifacts.








If you're looking to do some shopping, the Houston Galleria has tons of stores for you to visit.
If not then I would recommend you visit the Houston Zoo, tickets start around $20 depending on the day you choose to go.

Come Enjoy the Houston Rodeo!














Rodeo Info

The Houston Rodeo is only around for a limited time each year so if you're in Houston while it's happening i highly recommend you come out and enjoy the experience!
It's filled with everything from rides and events, to a carnival and food, all the way to live concerts!

If none of this excited you then you can always simply just roam around the city. Houston is truly a beautful place to just look at and enjoy!
